My hometown

I was born and raised in Chengdu, China. Chengdu is the capital of Sichuan, a province that locates southwest of China. For me, Chengdu not only raised the cutest animal of the world panda but also has the world’s best food such as Mapo toufu and hotpot. I lived in Chengdu for 20 years before I came to the United States. This city for me holds my previous memory of life.

My journey
  Go to my hometown   Go to my goal

My mom is a typical Chinese parent who first tried to shape my life into a pianist and later wanted me to follow her stepstone as a businesswoman. The pressure makes me rebellious, so I studied marine biology for my undergraduate degree.

My undegraduate life

After I started my own family, my life has shifted. I got involved into business to support my family financially and started loving it. I noticed good business creates a big impact for society and humanity. I learned that good leadership comes from listening instead of pushing. I enjoyed international business experience that allows me to connect with people from all over the world.

My goal

My goal is to become an impactful women leader and an example for my family. I want to build a business that is making the world a better place.
